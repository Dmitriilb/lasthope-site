// 01 — about

A place
that exists

Last Hope is not a concept. It's a result — of late nights, stray guitars, people who stayed too long and never really left.

Tucked in Đà Nẵng, it's a bar, a cafe, an exhibition space and an accidental community — plastered with art, lit by neon and held together by cold drinks and strangers who became regulars.
